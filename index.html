Alumnex

The Student-Alumni Network

Information Matters.

We want to help you find it.

Alumex is a social networking platform aimed at connecting current high school students with alumni who have graduated from their high school. Alumnex looks to connect students and alumni through student panels, networking events, and workshops. Alumnex is currently working within the St. Joan of Arc Catholic Secondary School community (Mississauga, Ontario) and is looking to expand to other high schools in the GTA in the coming years.
